Privacy Notice

How RydeQuest collects, uses, and protects your personal data across the GCC region.

Effective: February 2026 Version: 1.0 Last Updated: 27 February 2026

1. Who We Are

RydeQuest is an executive search and HR advisory firm operating across the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this notice.

2. Data We Collect

When you submit a CV, use our contact form, or otherwise interact with RydeQuest, we may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

CategoryExamples
Identity DataFull name, nationality, date of birth, photograph (if included in CV)
Contact DataEmail address, phone number, postal address
Professional DataWork history, education, qualifications, certifications, skills, references
Preference DataDesired role, salary expectations, location preferences, availability
Communication DataRecords of correspondence between you and RydeQuest
Technical DataIP address and browser information (when using our website)

We advise candidates not to include unnecessary sensitive information (such as health data, religious affiliation, or political opinions) in their CVs. Where sensitive data is provided, it will only be processed where strictly necessary or with your explicit additional consent.

3. How We Collect Your Data

Website Submission

When you submit your CV or enquiry through our website, you are required to actively confirm your consent by selecting the consent checkbox before the form can be submitted. This constitutes your informed, affirmative consent to the processing described in this notice.

Email Submission

When you email your CV to us (e.g., to careers@rydequest.com or hello@rydequest.com), you will receive an automated acknowledgement email containing this Privacy Notice. By submitting your CV via email, you consent to the processing of your personal data as described herein. You have a 14-day opt-out window from the date of the acknowledgement email to withdraw consent and request deletion of your data.

Third-Party Platforms

Where we receive your CV from job boards, recruitment platforms, or referral partners, we will send you a direct notification within 5 business days providing this Privacy Notice and a 14-day opt-out window.

4. Consent Through Submission

By submitting your CV or enquiry to RydeQuest through any channel, you expressly consent to the processing of your personal data as described in this Privacy Notice.

Your consent covers the following processing activities:

  • Storage of your CV and associated data in our secure candidate database
  • Review and assessment of your profile for current and future opportunities
  • Sharing your profile with our clients for candidate-role matching (with additional specific consent before sharing with named clients)
  • Contacting you about relevant job opportunities, industry insights, and our services
  • Anonymised, aggregated analysis for market insights and reporting
  • Retention as specified in our data retention schedule

6. Who We Share Your Data With

We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:

  • Our Clients: Organisations seeking to fill roles. We will seek additional specific consent before sharing your profile with a named client.
  • Service Providers: IT hosting, CRM systems, assessment platforms, and other processors acting on our behalf under Data Processing Agreements.
  • Regulatory Authorities: Where required by law in any GCC jurisdiction.
  • Professional Advisors: Legal, audit, and compliance advisors where necessary.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

7. Cross-Border Data Transfers

As a GCC-wide firm, your data may be transferred between our offices in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, and Oman. Where your data is transferred outside the jurisdiction in which it was collected, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses and Intra-Group Data Transfer Agreements, in compliance with local data protection laws.

9. Your Rights

Under GCC data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

RightWhat It Means

ErasureRequest deletion of your data where it is no longer needed
RestrictionRequest that we limit the processing of your data

Withdraw ConsentWithdraw your consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@rydequest.com. We will respond within 30 calendar days.

10. Withdrawing Your Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Emailing privacy@rydequest.com with your request
  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in any communication we send you
  • Replying to the auto-acknowledgement email within 14 days of your submission

Upon withdrawal, we will cease processing your data within 15 business days, securely delete your records from active systems, and send you written confirmation. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

11. How We Protect Your Data

We implement robust technical and organisational security measures, including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
  • Role-based access controls with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security assessments and annual penetration testing
  • Secure data disposal procedures for all expired records

12. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify the relevant regulatory authority and you directly in accordance with applicable GCC data protection laws. We will inform you of the nature of the breach, the likely consequences, and the measures we have taken to address it.
